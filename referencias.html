Barnes, J. (1982). The presocratic philosophers (2nd ed.). Routledge & Kegan Paul.

Guthrie, W. K. C. (1965). A history of Greek philosophy, Vol. II: The presocratic tradition from Parmenides to Democritus. Cambridge University Press.

Kahn, C. H. (1973). The verb 'be' in ancient Greek. Reidel.

Kirk, G. S., Raven, J. E., & Schofield, M. (1983). The presocratic philosophers (2nd ed.). Cambridge University Press.

Long, A. A. (Ed.). (1999). The Cambridge companion to early Greek philosophy. Cambridge University Press.

McKirahan, R. (2010). Philosophy before Socrates: An introduction with texts and commentaries (2nd ed.). Hackett Publishing.

Mourelatos, A. P. D. (2008). The route of Parmenides: A study of word, image, and argument in the fragments (Rev. and expanded ed.). Parmenides Publishing.

Palmer, J. A. (2009). Parmenides and presocratic philosophy. Oxford University Press.

Este glosario refleja los conceptos fundamentales que emergen con mayor claridad del análisis de García Morente sobre la metafísica parmenídea, manteniendo rigor académico y profundidad conceptual en cada entrada.
